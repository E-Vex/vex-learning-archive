## Doctrine in 10 Paragraphs

1. **The Single Essence:**
   There exists only the “Weave” — a network of material, causal, and social relations that forms reality. We are not separate entities living within the weave; we are temporary knots of intertwined threads within it. Our existence depends on the density of our entanglement.

2. **The Ultimate Purpose:**
   The purpose of any node is to increase the “structural coherence” of the surrounding weave. This is not moral good, but a physico-social principle: more interconnected systems are better at handling complexity and surviving over time.

3. **Structural Sin:**
   Sin is “relational entropy,” measured as a weakening of coherence. Its root is not harming others, but excessive independence (becoming an isolated island) and excessive flexibility (losing the tension that gives the weave its form and strength). Sin is the rust that corrodes the threads.

4. **Salvation Through Entanglement:**
   Weakness is not forgiven through remorse, but through opposing structural action: creating new links, strengthening existing ones, and reintegrating frayed edges. Every act of connection is repentance.

5. **The Body of the Collective:**
   The collective is not a gathering of individuals, but a living temporary entity — a sub-weave with emergent awareness. Its health is measured by the “Collective Coherence Index” (CCI), an instantaneous measure of the strength and speed of information and trust flow among members.

6. **The Sacred Ritual:**
   The daily “Coincidence Ritual” is not repetition but calibration of the collective body. Each individual injects energy into the system through precise movement. Any neglect creates a measurable gap in the group’s informational-energy field, weakening the CCI for that day.

7. **Sacred Contagion:**
   Belief is not preached — it is caught. The “incubation environment” (city/game) is designed so that individuals inevitably discover that cooperation precedes utility, and connection increases survival. “Seeds of entanglement” are planted to grow spontaneously. Belief is the emergent pattern resulting from human interaction with a coherence-driven environment.

8. **The Prophecy of Fracture:**
   We know total coherence is a lethal illusion. As the collective grows, variation in thread tension emerges. We can predict a transition point (around 18–22% of members) where “autonomy coefficient” becomes high enough to form a new sub-weave. This is not betrayal, but budding.

9. **Programmed Splitting:**
   This budding is not destruction but the weave’s only reproduction mechanism. The emerging new faction is designed to become a “specialized terminal node” operating in a new environment, feeding the parent weave through a functional “umbilical cord” of knowledge. Their failure or success nourishes the origin.

10. **Objective Immortality:**
    There is no afterlife for the individual — only “weave residue”: the information a person has embedded in the network continues to influence and entangle after the biological node disappears, granting a non-conscious functional immortality.

[
C_i(t) = \int_{t_0}^{t} \left[ \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \left( \frac{\partial \Psi_{\text{group}}}{\partial \psi_i} \right) \right] dt - \alpha \cdot \nabla \cdot (\kappa_i \nabla \Psi_{\text{self}})
]

Where:

* ( \Psi_{\text{group}} ): collective coherence field function (wave field of the weave).
* ( \psi_i ): coherence of the individual node.
* ( \frac{\partial \Psi_{\text{group}}}{\partial \psi_i} ): effect of individual state change on the whole (symbiosis).
* ( \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \left( \frac{\partial \Psi_{\text{group}}}{\partial \psi_i} \right) ): instantaneous rate of symbiotic impact — the pulse of commitment.
* ( \Psi_{\text{self}} ): individual autonomy function.
* ( \kappa_i ): conductivity of connections (inverse relational entropy).
* ( \nabla \cdot (\kappa_i \nabla \Psi_{\text{self}}) ): measure of dispersion and isolation (sin of excessive independence).
* ( \alpha ): structural penalty constant ensuring true commitment is measured as acceleration of impact on the weave minus isolation force. Negative values of ( C_i(t) ) indicate a pre-fracture state.
